khud se khuda tk

Khud se Khuda tak book is written by Muhammad Nasir Iftikhar. It is a novel, divided into three parts, subdivided into thirty-six chapters and each chapter has a different topic. This book contains sound knowledge about human nafs, their traps, and their solutions.
