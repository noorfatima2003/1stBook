Peer-E-Kamil / پیرِ کامل

Umaira Ahmed


The book deals with the turning points in intervening lives of two people: a runaway girl named Imama Hashim; and a boy named Salar Sikander with an IQ of more than 150. The story spans a time period of around ten years. It is Ahmad's most popular work. It is followed by a sequel, Aab-e-Hayat. Peer-e-Kamil novel was first published in 2004 in Shua Digest. Umera Ahmed named it Peer-e-Kamil which means 'The Perfect Mentor' that signifies the character of the Holy Prophet A novel is a relatively long work of narrative fiction, typically written in prose and published as a book.This beautiful novel 'PEER-E-KAMIL' is written by Umaira Ahmad and it is about eternal love, the love of Allah Almighty, that results from the love of the Holy Prophet (peace be upon him) .

525 pages, Hardcover

First published March 1, 2012
